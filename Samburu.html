Samburu National Reserve

The Samburu National Reserve is a game reserve on the banks of the Ewaso Ng'iro river in Kenya.This vast expanse of remote pristine wilderness measures 165 square kilometers in area and borders the Ewaso Ng'iro River to the south, which separates it from the Buffalo Springs National Reserve.
Being a one day safari for us(my family),we saw many animals yet of few species.
Mainly elephants were most prevalent at the time of our drive;this is because the reserve is a home to about 900 elephants.
