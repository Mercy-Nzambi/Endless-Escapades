WELCOME TO THE BEAUTIFUL CITY OF NAKURU

A memorable journal of the beautiful scenary of Nakuru

This being the first time i went to nakuru, the following are the best memories

A VISIT TO LAKE NAKURU

That day early in the morning my friend and i woke up early to prepare ourselves for the journey. Everything seemed normal until we boarded our jeep and embarked on a beautiful trip to lake nakuru. The moment our eyes flashed across the lake, we were blown off the beautiful sight of the lake and the colourful flamingos.We stayed the whole day watching the beautiful birds as they took off and others landed sliding on the surface of the water.We recorded and took beautiful photos of the birds.

Lord Egerton castle

Lord Egerton Castle is a house styled like a fortress located 14km outside Nakuru It is one of the places i visited during my stay at Nakuru and had a great time. it was beautiful and the point of difference

Hell's Gate National Park

Hell's gate National Park is a park located between lake naivasha and the longonot and suswa volcanoes. It is covered by ashes from the longonot eruption which occured 100 years ago,the park is famous for its geothermal station and spectacular scenaries.

Hyrax Hill Museum

Hyrax Hill is popular with hikers and adventures.It is ideal for nature walks, camping picns, photography and archaelogical research.
